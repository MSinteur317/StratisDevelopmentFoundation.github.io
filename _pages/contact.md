# Contacting the Stratis Development Foundation

Members of the SDF can be contacted by [email](mailto:stratisdevelopmentfoundation@gmail.com), or by [Telegram](https://t.me/joinchat/FM9Hq1HMf3tKxxeJUB2M3w).
